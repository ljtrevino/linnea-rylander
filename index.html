Street Smarts  

StreetSmarts is a series of smart street lights that can be used to map optimal routes emergency responders should through take though a city. When the lamp unit detects motion, it begins listening for voice commands and uses voice recognition to identify if there is an emergency and contact the nearest fire department if there is. At the fire station, a firefighter will accept the request for help and send a team of firefighters to help. After the server runs an algorithm to determine the quickest route to the emergency location, the street lamps along that route light up. This helps the firefighters know where to go, and also alerts drivers along the way that an emergency vehicle will be coming soon so that they can clear the way for the emergency vehicle. In record time, the firefighters can reach the emergency location and save lives.
